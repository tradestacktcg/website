Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

TradeStack ("the app") is operated by Malick Jarrett ("I", "me"). This policy explains what information the app accesses and how it is used.

Information I Collect

TradeStack does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require an account, login, or any personally identifiable information to function.

The app is session-only. No trade data, card history, or usage information is saved between sessions or transmitted to any server controlled by me.

Third-Party Services

TradeStack connects to two external services to function:

  • pokemontcg.io — provides card search, images, and set data. Requests include card search terms you enter. Review their privacy policy at pokemontcg.io.
  • PokemonPriceTracker.com — provides card pricing data. Requests include card identifiers used to retrieve prices. Review their privacy policy at pokemonpricetracker.com.

These services may log requests in accordance with their own privacy policies. I do not control their data practices.

Children's Privacy

TradeStack does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If I add analytics, crash reporting, or other data collection in a future version, this policy will be updated before that version ships. The "last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
